# SAML Single Sign-On for Enterprise

WordLift supports SAML-based single sign-on (SSO) for Enterprise customers. With SAML SSO, your organization can let users access WordLift through a supported identity provider instead of managing a separate WordLift-specific login flow.

SAML SSO is available for Enterprise customers who need centralized authentication, stronger access governance, and an identity workflow that fits existing enterprise security policies.

## What is SAML?

SAML, or Security Assertion Markup Language, is an open standard used to exchange authentication and authorization data between an identity provider and a service provider. In an enterprise SSO flow, the identity provider authenticates the user and sends WordLift the information needed to grant access.

In this relationship:

- The identity provider (IdP) manages user authentication for your organization.
- WordLift acts as the service provider (SP) that receives the authenticated identity.
- Your users sign in through the enterprise identity system they already use.

## Supported identity providers

WordLift supports SAML integration with enterprise identity providers, including:

- Okta
- Microsoft Entra ID
- Google Workspace

If your organization uses another SAML-compatible identity provider, contact us so that we can confirm the best setup path for your Enterprise account.

## Why SAML SSO matters for Enterprise teams

SAML single sign-on helps Enterprise customers align WordLift access with their internal security and compliance processes.

Common benefits include:

- **Centralized access management:** manage user authentication through the same identity provider used across the organization.
- **Simplified onboarding and offboarding:** give teams access through established enterprise identity workflows.
- **Reduced password exposure:** limit the need for users to manage separate credentials for WordLift.
- **Better governance:** support internal policies for authentication, identity lifecycle management, and access control.

## WordLift SAML SSO for enterprise SEO operations

Enterprise SEO, content, product, and data teams often work across multiple tools, markets, websites, and agencies. SAML SSO helps these teams bring WordLift into the same identity framework used by the rest of the organization.

For Enterprise customers, this is especially useful when WordLift is part of a broader SEO and AI search stack that includes Knowledge Graph operations, structured data workflows, content generation, analytics integrations, and internal governance requirements.
